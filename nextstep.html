The AUB NextStep program website is a comprehensive platform designed to support students with disabilities in their learning journey and career path.

The website showcases the various features and offerings of the NextStep program, including specialized courses, employment services, and success stories of students who have benefitted from the program.

Features ðŸŒŸ

  • Dynamic Page Design: Utilized WPBakery Page Builder and Elementor to create visually appealing and functional pages.
  • Image Galleries: Integrated Envira Gallery Lite to manage and display images, enhancing the visual appeal of the website.
  • Event Management: Implemented the Event Manager and Tickets Selling Plugin for WooCommerce - WpEvently to manage and showcase events, allowing users to view and participate in program-related activities.
  • Testimonials: Used the Real Testimonials plugin to display positive feedback from parents, directors, and other stakeholders, highlighting the program's impact.
  • Homepage Sliders: Leveraged Slider Revolution to create engaging sliders on the homepage, capturing the essence of the program.
  • Team Profiles: Built the "About Us" section using the Team Builder for WPBakery Page Builder, showcasing the dedicated team behind the program.
  • Donations and Payments: Integrated WooCommerce to facilitate donations and payment methods, supporting the program financially.
  • Security: Enhanced website security using the Wordfence Security plugin to protect user data and ensure a safe browsing experience.
  • Job Openings: Added the WP Job Openings plugin to list job vacancies within the program, such as special educators, enabling easy recruitment.

Content highlights include:

  • Success Stories: Featured stories of disabled individuals who have secured paid jobs through the program, demonstrating its effectiveness and impact.
  • Internships Section: Provided information on internship opportunities where students gain practical experience at various companies and program partners, preparing them for future employment.
  • Curriculum and Courses: Detailed the program's curriculum, including courses like Art Therapy, Employment Skills, Daily Living Skills, Functional Math and Money Management, Self-Determination and Interpersonal Skills, and Dance Movement Therapy.
  • News and Articles: Regularly updated with news and articles about the program, keeping visitors informed about recent developments and achievements.
  • Contact Forms: Created and managed contact forms to facilitate communication between users and the program administration.
